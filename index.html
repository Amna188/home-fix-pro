FAQ Questions & Answers

Are there any inspection or visit charges?

We try to keep basic inspections free. However, if diagnosing the issue takes time (such as locating a hidden water leakage) a minimal inspection fee may apply. This fee is fully adjusted into your final bill once the work begins.

Who will provide the materials (pipes, wires, paint, etc.)?

 It is completely up to you. You can purchase the materials yourself or our expert technicians can source high-quality materials for you and provide the official receipt/bill.

Is there a warranty on AC servicing or repair work?

 Yes, we offer a limited-time warranty on our AC services and electrical repairs. If the same issue arises shortly after our service our team will fix it free of charge.

Can I book a service for the same day in an emergency?

Absolutely! For urgent issues like severe plumbing leakages, short circuits, or AC cooling failures, we prioritize same-day emergency bookings to resolve your problem as quickly as possible.

How do I book a service with HomeFixPro?

Booking is simple! You can click the “Book Service” button at the top of our website to fill out a quick form or you can call/ us directly at our provided number.
